Frequently Asked Questions

Is Omni a replacement for traditional therapy?

No, Omni is a wellness companion designed to support your mental health journey. While it uses evidence-based techniques, it's not a substitute for licensed therapy or emergency services. If you're in crisis, please contact emergency services immediately.

How private are my conversations?

Your privacy is our top priority. All conversations are encrypted and stored securely on your device. We never share your personal data or therapy sessions with third parties.

What therapeutic techniques does Omni use?

Omni is built on cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT), mindfulness practices, and other evidence-based psychological techniques proven to help with anxiety, stress, and emotional wellness.

Can I try Omni before subscribing?

Yes! All plans include a free trial period so you can experience Omni's full features before committing to a subscription.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime through your App Store account settings. Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

When will voice support be available?

Voice-to-voice support is currently in development and will be available soon. Pro subscribers will get early access when it launches.
