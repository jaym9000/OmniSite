Frequently Asked Questions

Is Omni a replacement for traditional therapy?

No, Omni is a wellness companion designed to support your mental health journey. While it uses evidence-based techniques and GPT-4 technology, it's not a substitute for licensed therapy or emergency services. If you're in crisis, please contact emergency services immediately.

How does the token system work?

You start with 50 free tokens. Each chat message costs 2 tokens and voice sessions cost 5 tokens. Journaling and mood tracking are always free. Tokens never expire, or you can get unlimited usage with a subscription.

Is voice therapy really available now?

Yes! Real-time voice therapy is live and available to all users. Choose from 11 different therapeutic voices and talk naturally with your AI therapist anytime you need support.

How private are my conversations?

Your privacy is our top priority. All conversations are encrypted and stored securely. We never track or share your conversations with anyone. Your therapy sessions stay completely private and confidential.

What's the difference between plans?

Free gives you 50 tokens to try. Pay-as-you-go lets you buy tokens ($4.99-$24.99) that never expire. Unlimited ($19.99/month or $99.99/year) gives unlimited chat, unlimited voice, all 11 voices, and advanced analytics. Annual saves $139/year.

Can I try before I buy?

Absolutely! You get 50 free tokens to start (no credit card required). This gives you approximately 25 chat messages or 10 voice sessions to experience Omni's features before deciding to purchase more tokens or subscribe.

What therapeutic techniques does Omni use?

Omni is built on cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT), mindfulness practices, and other evidence-based psychological techniques. It provides intelligent, empathetic support with built-in safety features to help you on your mental health journey.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime through your App Store account settings. Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period. There are no cancellation fees or penalties.
